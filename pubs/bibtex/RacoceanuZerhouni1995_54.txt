In this paper we expound a new reduction method for ergodic classes formed by such persistent states. An ergodic class has a steady-state independent of the initial distribution. This class constitutes an irreducible finite ergodic Markov chain, which evolves independently after the capture of the event.The reduction is made according to the significance of steady-state probabilities.
